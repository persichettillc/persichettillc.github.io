Fractional Chief Actuary Services

Senior actuarial leadership when the decision matters—but a full-time hire does not.

What this solves

Organizations often reach a point where actuarial input is no longer transactional. Strategic decisions require executive-level actuarial judgment, independent validation, and a decision framework that boards and finance leaders can defend.

  • Strategic decisions are being made with incomplete actuarial framing
  • Internal teams are strong technically but need senior judgment and governance support
  • Advisors, carriers, or vendors bring incentives that obscure trade-offs
  • Leadership wants independent validation—not another model run

What you receive

  • Executive decision support: clear trade-offs across cost, risk, competitiveness, and compliance
  • Strategic actuarial leadership: benefit strategy, risk-based pricing, sustainability analysis
  • Independent oversight: review and challenge carrier/TPA/consultant assumptions and projections
  • Fiduciary-ready documentation: rationale suitable for board review, audit, and governance needs

Who this is for

  • Self-funded employers facing sustained cost pressure or fiduciary scrutiny
  • Provider-sponsored plans balancing care delivery and insurance risk
  • Health systems and risk-bearing entities launching or restructuring arrangements
  • Boards and executives seeking an independent actuarial perspective

Engagement structure

  • Monthly retainer aligned to decision cycles
  • Fixed-scope support tied to a defined strategic decision
  • Interim leadership during transitions, launches, or restructuring
