Fractional Chief Actuary Services

Senior actuarial leadership when the decision matters—but a full-time hire does not.

I provide fractional actuarial leadership to health tech and venture-backed healthcare organizations that price, manage, or assume healthcare cost risk without full-time actuarial executive infrastructure.

My role is to ensure pricing logic, risk assumptions, and economic narratives remain credible under scale, investor scrutiny, and regulatory exposure—while preserving independence from revenue-aligned vendors.

This model delivers Chief Actuary-level judgment and governance when it matters most, without the cost or rigidity of a full-time executive hire.

What this solves

  • Investor-facing pricing narratives that do not survive diligence without actuarial rigor
  • Unit economics and risk assumptions that degrade as membership scales
  • Risk arrangements (capitation, bundles, downside risk) without executive actuarial governance
  • Regulatory, audit, or contract exposure where assumptions must be defensible
  • Vendor or partner incentives that bias outcomes and obscure trade-offs

How I help

  • Pricing logic & risk assumptions: pressure-test inputs, credibility, and sensitivity; define guardrails
  • Economic narrative under scrutiny: translate actuarial uncertainty into board- and investor-ready rationale
  • Governance & decision support: build decision frameworks executives can use repeatedly, not one-off models
  • Independent validation: review partner/vendor analyses without revenue alignment or implementation bias
  • Documentation: diligence-ready artifacts suitable for audit, contracting, and governance

Engagement structure

  • Monthly retainer aligned to decision cycles and governance needs
  • Fixed-scope support tied to a defined pricing, contracting, or financing decision
  • Interim Chief Actuary leadership during rapid growth, pivots, or executive transitions
