About Persichetti LLC

Persichetti LLC is an independent actuarial consulting practice focused on employer-sponsored health plans and related health system stakeholders. With more than 25 years of experience, I help employers, providers, and advisors navigate complex questions around costs, risk, and regulation.

My work spans traditional actuarial analysis, transparency and fiduciary governance under the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2021, and practical, data-driven strategies that connect plan design, pricing, and member experience.
